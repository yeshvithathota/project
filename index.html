Expert Advice

Resume & Career Tips

Insights from hiring managers, recruiters, and career coaches to maximise your chances.

TIP — 01

Tailor Every Application

Generic resumes get ignored. Spend 10 minutes customising keywords from the job description — recruiters spot the difference immediately.

TIP — 02

Lead with Impact Numbers

"Increased sales by 40%" beats "responsible for sales." Quantify your achievements wherever possible — even in non-numeric roles.

TIP — 03

One Page Isn't Always the Rule

Entry-level: one page. Mid-career: one or two. Senior / Academic: as long as needed. Quality over brevity — but never pad.

TIP — 04

Beat the ATS Bots

Most companies use Applicant Tracking Systems. Use standard section headings, avoid tables and images, and include exact keywords from the job post.

TIP — 05

Your LinkedIn Must Match

Recruiters will cross-reference your LinkedIn profile. Ensure dates, titles, and descriptions are consistent — discrepancies raise red flags.

TIP — 06

Action Verbs Open Doors

Start every bullet with a strong action verb: Architected, Spearheaded, Negotiated, Mentored, Deployed. Avoid passive constructions.

TIP — 07

Address Career Gaps Honestly

Employment gaps aren't disqualifying if explained. Freelancing, caregiving, upskilling — frame gaps as intentional growth periods.

TIP — 08

References: Available on Request

Don't list references on your resume — it wastes space. Prepare 3 strong references separately and have them ready when asked.

TIP — 09

Proofread Three Times

Spelling errors are the fastest way to the reject pile. Read aloud, use a tool, then give it to someone else. Never skip this step.

Getting Started
🚀 How do I create an account?▼
Click Register in the top navigation. Fill in your name, email, career stream, and password. You can also sign up instantly with Google or LinkedIn.

✏️ How do I update my profile?▼
After logging in, click your avatar in the top right and select My Profile. You can update your name, email, career stream, and profile photo at any time.
Resume Templates
📄 How do I download a resume?▼
Select a template, click Use This, fill in your details in the editor, then click Download PDF. Free templates export with a CareerForge watermark; premium templates are watermark-free.
🤖 Are templates ATS-friendly?▼
Yes! All templates marked ATS Safe use single-column layouts, standard fonts, and no tables or images — ensuring they pass Applicant Tracking Systems used by 95% of employers.
🆓 Which templates are free?▼
Templates marked with a FREE badge are fully free — Classic Ivory, Tech Midnight, Clean Minimal, and Academic CV. Premium templates require a CareerForge Pro subscription.
Career Guidance
🧭 How does the career quiz work?▼
Our aptitude quiz takes about 8 minutes and analyses your personality, interests, and skills across 5 dimensions. You'll get a ranked list of career streams with personalised roadmaps for each.
👩‍💼 How do I book a counselor session?▼
Scroll to the Career Counselors section and click Book a Session on any counselor's card. Choose a date and time, complete payment, and you'll receive a confirmation with a meeting link.
💬 What can CareerBot help with?▼
CareerBot (the AI chat widget at the bottom right) can answer questions about career streams, resume writing, entrance exams, course recommendations, and interview tips — available 24/7 for instant advice.
